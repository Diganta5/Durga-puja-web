About Durga Puja

Durga Puja is one of the most significant festivals in India, celebrating the victory of the goddess Durga over the demon Mahishasura. It is a celebration of divine power and the triumph of good over evil. Every year, millions of devotees come together to worship the goddess, perform rituals, and participate in cultural activities.
